Welcome to the Home Page

This is a webpage about one of the sportsball organizations that the University of Colorado exploits for the purpose of advertising its campus to potential student recruits from out of state. Since out of state studets are required to pay higher tuition, the administartion (as well as the administrations of many other state schools) has long ago determined that maximizing out of state students is the ideal way to grow the Univeristiy. This particular varient of sportsball is very popular in the USA, in fact, some people consider it to be the de facto national religion. Clearly, this is the ideal children's game to bolster recruitment numbers! Here is a link to the official website of the CU Buffs, which I think is short for Buffalo, a type of North American land mammal that used to exist in Boulder but had to be forcefully removed from the area before construction crews could break ground on Old Main in 1876.
